FROM THE PAST, TO THE FUTURE

Focusing On What Matters Most

Ultimately, the purpose of technology is to make people's lives better. And I'm searching for an opportunity where I can do just that!

In late 2015, I became passionately interested in philosophy. I wanted to understand what it is we're all here for, and what we should do with our lives. I arrived at the conclusion that each individual's ultimate imperative is to make the biggest postive impact on the world that they can, with whatever resources they have.

Inspired by this realization, I aimed to ground my education with a rigorous program that could give me general yet difficult to acquire skills that I can apply towards any problem. So I chose to study mathematics, with a minor in econonmics, and graduated in 2021.

With my degree in hand, I sought to begin my career as a data analyst to build the technical skills necessary for solving challenging real-world problems. I was hired as a data analyst by a small AI startup called Intelligent Artifacts, which aimed to improve the world by building explainable and interpretable AI. I was intrigued by the company's goal and ultimately worked as a solutions engineer for the company for three years, until it shut down in April of this year.

Now I'm searching for my next opportunity. Having worked on AI for 3 years, my focus now is to ensure that AI is built in such a way that ultimately benefits humanity.
